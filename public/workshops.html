FIRST AAUREX WORKSHOP

Kidney Dissection Workshop — The Beginning of AAUREX Practical Learning

AAUREX FIRST EVENT

Not just a workshop — a new way to experience medicine.

The Kidney Dissection Workshop will be the first practical event by AAUREX, created to make medical learning more exciting, interactive, and unforgettable. Instead of simply listening to theory, students will experience kidney anatomy through practical observation, creative teaching, clinical connection, and different forms of art.

Our aim is to show students that medicine is not only something to memorise. It can be seen, modelled, acted, explained, visualised, discussed, performed, designed, and experienced.

This workshop will combine anatomy with creativity so that students understand the kidney in a deeper and more enjoyable way.

Date: 21 June 2026
Time: 12:00 PM – 5:00 PM
Event Type: Hands-on Anatomy & Creative Medical Learning Workshop
Focus: Kidney Dissection, Renal Anatomy, Art-Based Learning & Clinical Concepts

WHAT MAKES THE FIRST EVENT SPECIAL

Medicine taught through dissection, creativity, memory, and imagination.

Hands-On Kidney Dissection

Students will explore the kidney through practical dissection-based learning. They will observe the external surface, internal arrangement, major renal structures, anatomical landmarks, and important features that are usually difficult to understand only from textbook diagrams.

Art in Many Forms

In AAUREX, art does not only mean drawing or sketching. We will use visual storytelling, model-based explanation, role-play, medical theatre, rhythm-based mnemonics, concept mapping, digital visuals, creative diagrams, and memory techniques to make anatomy easier to understand.

AI-Generated Concept Visuals

The event will include AI-inspired medical visuals and concept-based illustrations to help students imagine kidney structure, blood supply, filtration, and clinical relevance in a more modern and engaging way.

Clinical Connection

Students will not only learn what the kidney looks like, but also why it matters clinically. The session will connect renal anatomy with medicine, diagnosis, kidney function, blood supply, and common clinical understanding needed by future doctors.

CREATIVE MEDICAL LEARNING

A premium workshop experience created for medical students.

Visual Anatomy

Kidney anatomy will be explained through visual memory, structure mapping, labelled understanding, and simple creative breakdowns.

Medical Storytelling

Concepts will be explained like a story, helping students connect anatomy, function, blood supply, and clinical relevance.

AI Concept Visuals

Modern AI-style visuals will support learning by turning complex anatomy into clearer, more imaginative concept images.

Clinical Connection

Every creative explanation will be connected back to medicine, patient care, diagnosis, and practical understanding.
