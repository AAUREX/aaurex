The vision behind AAUREX.

AAUREX Founder & President

Tarun Murali Mandadi

Founder, President & Lead Visionary of AAUREX

Tarun Murali Mandadi is the President and Founder of AAUREX, leading the organisation with a vision to transform medical learning into something more practical, creative, research-oriented, and student-driven. He founded AAUREX to create a strong platform where medical students can learn beyond textbooks, participate in workshops, explore research, attend academic events, and build confidence as future healthcare professionals.

His role includes guiding the overall direction of AAUREX, building the organisation’s digital presence, planning academic initiatives, coordinating team structure, developing workshop ideas, and creating systems that allow students to grow through leadership, teamwork, communication, and innovation.

Through AAUREX, Tarun aims to connect medical education with creativity, technology, research, and practical exposure. His focus is to build a professional student-led medical ecosystem where members feel inspired to contribute, learn, lead, and represent excellence.

Vision Making medical learning practical, creative, and accessible.
Leadership Guiding teams, events, research, workshops, and digital growth.
Innovation Combining medicine with creativity, technology, and AI learning tools.
Mission Building a strong student-led medical organisation with purpose.
