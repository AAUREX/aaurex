Upcoming AAUREX Event

Kidney Dissection Workshop 2026

AAUREX presents an immersive hands-on Kidney Dissection Workshop designed for medical students and young healthcare learners to understand renal anatomy through practical exposure, guided explanation, creative teaching, and clinical connection.

This workshop will help participants explore the external and internal features of the kidney, understand renal blood supply, identify important anatomical landmarks, and connect textbook anatomy with real practical observation.

Hands-on Anatomy Practical renal dissection with guided learning.
Creative Learning Medicine taught through visuals, memory, storytelling, models, and art.
